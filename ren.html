Based on some initial conversations with REN we believe that the following packages are of interest for you.

Community of Practice - Practical knowledge sharing

Knowledge sharing is a common problem in many companies. Software development is complex and requires a lot of effort and time. Knowledge is valuable and must be shared between all the teams sharing a technology platform. Oikosofy can plan and implement a Community Of Practice (CoP) approach where knowledge sharing can become a natural process in your organization. Through this approach we can help you start and develop your Community of Practice, keeping the knowledge in house.

Evaluate code quality risk and devise quality strategy

Technical Debt is a major threat to a stable and continuous business. Technical Debt can slow down your development process and make changes to your applications slow and difficult. Oikosofy's experience in Agile software development is a key asset in detecting and tackling Technical Debt. We can provide a limited package that focuses on evaluating risks in your code and devise a quality strategy together with your technical teams. Ensuring that in the long term you will not be slowed down by the accumulated Technical Debt.

Internal training program with experience sharing

Keeping up with the latest developments in software development field takes time and effort. Being up to date on the latest technology developments is key to be able to keep up the speed of innovation. Oikosofy is your partner in reseraching the latest innovations in software development, project management and knowledge work management. An internal training program is a very cost-effective way to keep your teams up to date with the latest development, and share the learnings collected from different teams. Knowledge is valuable, investing in creating and keeping knowledge in house always pays back.

Lessons learned facilitation

Oikosofy is your partner in facilitating energizing and engaging lessons learned sessions. Learning is the enabler for change and improvement.

Project management audting and improvement suggestions

It is easy to get caught up in the repetition. Processes need to change to adapt to new reality.
